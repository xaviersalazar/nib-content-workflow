## Fly Ranch and its iconic Fly Geyser has quickly become one of Nevada's premier outdoor destinations.

Throughout the course of this 3-hour guided nature-walk you'll learn about the region's history and ecology, all while being delighted by its many unique sights: from large-scale and interactive Burning Man art-pieces, to wild horses, migrating birds, and indomitable landscapes, all culminating in a breathtaking and close-up view of Fly Geyser.

All proceeds from these Nature Walks directly benefit our conservation work in the Black Rock Desert-High Rock Canyon National Conservation Area. Make a day of it and learn more about what makes this region so special to so many.
