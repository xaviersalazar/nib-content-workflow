# About Coffee

We believe that coffee is more than just a drink: It’s a culture, an economy, an art, a science — and a passion.

![](https://www.ncausa.org/portals/56/Images/Industry/NCA_Web_Medium_sprouts.png?ver=2016-05-20-132935-680)

#### [visit aboutcoffee.org](https://www.ncausa.org/About-Coffee/Visit-About-Coffee-Website)

A guide to our consumer website all about coffee—from its origins to the different types of coffee, brewing methods, independent research on coffee and health, and more.


![hand picking ripe coffee cherries](https://www.ncausa.org/portals/56/Images/AboutCoffee/sustain%20showcase%20thumbnail.jpg)

#### [NCA Sustainability Showcase](https://www.ncausa.org/Sustainability-Showcase)

We are proud to recognize the important work that NCA members are doing across the sector, from improving the conditions at origin to developing eco-friendly facilities and recyclable packaging materials.


![](https://www.ncausa.org/portals/56/Images/AboutCoffee/coffee%20gives%20back%20thumbnail.jpg)

#### [NCA Coffee Gives Back Charity Showcase](https://www.ncausa.org/About-Coffee/Coffee-Gives-Back-Showcase)

As part of the NCA Coffee Gives Back program, this charity showcase amplifies the visibility of organizations dedicated to supporting coffee origin communities.
