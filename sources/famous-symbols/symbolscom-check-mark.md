# Check mark

#### This page is about the meaning, origin and characteristic of the symbol, emblem, seal, sign, logo or flag: **Check mark**.

[![](https://static.stands4.com/images/user/2)](https://www.symbols.com/user/2 "Yigal Ben Efraim")

[Yigal Ben Efraim](https://www.symbols.com/user/2 "Yigal Ben Efraim")

Rate this symbol: [**3.3** / 7 votes](https://www.symbols.com/symbol-voters/2320)

![Check mark](https://static.stands4.com/images/symbol/1/2320_check-mark.png)

A check mark or tick is a symbol used to indicate the concept "yes".

The [checkmark](https://www.definitions.net/definition/checkmark) symbol, also [known](https://www.definitions.net/definition/known) as the tick symbol, is [believed](https://www.definitions.net/definition/believed) to have been [first](https://www.definitions.net/definition/first) used in the 1800s as a way to mark [items](https://www.definitions.net/definition/items) on a list as [completed](https://www.definitions.net/definition/completed) or verified. However, the [exact](https://www.definitions.net/definition/exact) date and [origin](https://www.definitions.net/definition/origin) of the [symbol](https://www.definitions.net/definition/symbol) are unknown. It has [since](https://www.definitions.net/definition/since) become a [widely](https://www.definitions.net/definition/widely) recognized [symbol](https://www.definitions.net/definition/symbol) for [indicating](https://www.definitions.net/definition/indicating) correctness or approval.

The [check](https://www.definitions.net/definition/check) mark is a [predominant](https://www.definitions.net/definition/predominant) affirmative [symbol](https://www.definitions.net/definition/symbol) of [convenience](https://www.definitions.net/definition/convenience) in the English-speaking [world](https://www.definitions.net/definition/world) because of its [instant](https://www.definitions.net/definition/instant) and [facile](https://www.definitions.net/definition/facile) composition. In [other](https://www.definitions.net/definition/other) countries, however, the mark is more complicated. In some [countries](https://www.definitions.net/definition/countries) (e.g., Finland, Italy, Japan, Korea, Norway, and Sweden), the [check](https://www.definitions.net/definition/check) mark can be used as an [error](https://www.definitions.net/definition/error) mark, [indicating](https://www.definitions.net/definition/indicating) "no" [rather](https://www.definitions.net/definition/rather) than "yes". For example, it is [common](https://www.definitions.net/definition/common) in [Swedish](https://www.definitions.net/definition/Swedish) schools for a ✓ to [indicate](https://www.definitions.net/definition/indicate) that an [answer](https://www.definitions.net/definition/answer) is incorrect.

In China, a [check](https://www.definitions.net/definition/check) mark [means](https://www.definitions.net/definition/means) "correct". A [check](https://www.definitions.net/definition/check) mark [placed](https://www.definitions.net/definition/placed) in [brackets](https://www.definitions.net/definition/brackets) can [indicate](https://www.definitions.net/definition/indicate) that a [previously](https://www.definitions.net/definition/previously) used or [acceptable](https://www.definitions.net/definition/acceptable) fact or [definition](https://www.definitions.net/definition/definition) is [being](https://www.definitions.net/definition/being) investigated, [usually](https://www.definitions.net/definition/usually) for the [purpose](https://www.definitions.net/definition/purpose) of [expanding](https://www.definitions.net/definition/expanding) academic research.

In Japan, an "O Mark", also [known](https://www.definitions.net/definition/known) as "丸印" (marujirushi), is used [instead](https://www.definitions.net/definition/instead) of a [check](https://www.definitions.net/definition/check) mark to mean "yes"; this [symbol](https://www.definitions.net/definition/symbol) is also used in [Korea](https://www.definitions.net/definition/Korea) and Taiwan.

The "Symbol, Other" [category](https://www.definitions.net/definition/category) of [Unicode](https://www.definitions.net/definition/Unicode) provides [various](https://www.definitions.net/definition/various) check marks:

U+237B ⍻ not [check](https://www.definitions.net/definition/check) mark

U+2610 ☐ [ballot](https://www.definitions.net/definition/ballot) box

U+2611 ☑ [ballot](https://www.definitions.net/definition/ballot) box with check

U+2705 ✅ [white](https://www.definitions.net/definition/white) heavy [check](https://www.definitions.net/definition/check) mark

U+2713 ✓ [check](https://www.definitions.net/definition/check) mark

U+2714 ✔ [heavy](https://www.definitions.net/definition/heavy) check mark
